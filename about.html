OUR STORY

South Side Rydaz M.C.

A group of professional and upstanding men and women who have a combined love for motorcycles, and most of all, their families and community.

Established in 2003, South Side Rydaz have been around for 20+ years strong. What started as a small group has grown into a respected brotherhood with 4 chapters:

  • Biloxi, MS (Mother Chapter)
  • Shreveport, LA
  • Alcorn, MS
  • Olive Branch, MS

And coming to a city near you!
